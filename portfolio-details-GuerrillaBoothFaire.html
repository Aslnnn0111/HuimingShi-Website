Logline

Guerrilla Booth Faire is a competitive strategy board building game that revolves around a Merchant booth, Theater booth, Bakery booth, and Sports booth. Players calculate all the points at the end and the player with the most points wins the game.

Overview

Guerrilla Booth Faire pits 4 players against each other in a race to build the biggest and best booth at the Renaissance Faire. The game is played by taking “booth” cards at random and using those assets as a way to build your booth at the Faire by placing tokens on the board. There are 4 different booths, Merchant, Sports, Bakery, and Theater, each with its own abilities. Players physically expand their booths on the board, aiming to try and occupy star card spaces to gain specific abilities or extra points.

Component

  • 1 game board
  • 40 booth cards
  • 20 ability cards
  • 24 star cards
  • 15 green, blue, gray, and red booth tokens
  • 4 green, blue, gray, and red ad tokens
  • Paper and pencil for scoring
