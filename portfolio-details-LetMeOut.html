Overview

This is a 3D escape room game. The story of this game kicks off with a tense moment as a frustrated mother scolds the protagonist, a child, for neglecting their studies. His being caught chatting with friends on cell phones instead of doing his homework.

The child faces the consequences as their phone is confiscated and the door is locked with wire. The key to unlocking the door is placed at a cabinet in the stairwell,a clear attempt by the mother to keep her child confined to the room.

However, with plans already made to meet his friend and go out to play, The child must navigate three challenging levels, evading their mother's watchful eye, to successfully escape through the main door.
