Overview

Re:Live is a puzzle game based on mechanics and supplemented by story. The game's mechanics will challenge the player's spatial imagination, logical thinking and path planning abilities. Players will control the same character in two space-time, by placing the modular design of the square map in the map, for the two space-time characters to plan a feasible route, find a way to reach the end.
