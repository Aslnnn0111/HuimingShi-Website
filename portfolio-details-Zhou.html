Logline

Explore the mysterious Chinese village, reveal dark truths behind supernatural occurrences.

Overview

"Zhou" is a 2D narrative-driven horror game set in the backdrop of Chinese folklore. Zhang Wangshu, lacking any memories prior to the age of seven, grew up in an orphanage from the time she could remember. However, for many years, she has been haunted by dreams about a mysterious ancient village. Vaguely, she feels a deep connection between herself and this place. This longing eventually leads her to become a folklorist and secure a position as a columnist in the city. One day, the chief editor of the column received an invitation from an ancient village, requesting a researcher to visit and explore their village. This opportunity ultimately fell into the hands of Zhang Wangshu, who was passionate about studying ancient villages. And thus, the story of the prologue begins.
