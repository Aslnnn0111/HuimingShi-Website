Overview

This is a two-player cooperative game in which two players need to control two extending tree roots from two endpoints at the top of the screen, down through obstacles, and finally successfully meet at the bottom of the screen. In the process of traversing obstacles, there will be some switches that need to be triggered by the player, which will open the corresponding door; Some collectible elements also appear. During the player's movement, the player can choose to stop the current growth of the root, and go back to a part of the root to grow a new branch. The game wants to express a sense of connection between people, and the roots of each other's obstruction, temptation overlap, and finally meet is the process of this connection.
